Keyboard Shortcuts & Tips

Formatting

⌘B or CtrlB Bold text
⌘I or CtrlI Italic text
/ Insert block (type / at start of line)
- Space Create bullet list (type at line start)
Tab Indent list items
ShiftTab Outdent list items

Navigation & Editing

⌘S or CtrlS Save (auto-saves in background)
⌘A or CtrlA Select all text
⌘C or CtrlC Copy selected text

Features

Word Count Set a target to track your progress. Click the + button next to word count.
Compare Mode Paste text on the right to compare two versions side-by-side.
Posts Create multiple posts and switch between them. Click the document icon on the left.
Auto-save Your work is saved automatically every 5 seconds and when you switch posts.
Dark Mode Toggle dark mode from the menu for comfortable writing in low light.
