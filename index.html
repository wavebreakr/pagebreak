The PASTOR Framework
A copywriting formula for content that actually converts
P
Problem
Identify and name the exact pain your reader is experiencing. Be specific — the more precisely you describe their problem, the more they feel understood.
A
Amplify
Deepen the stakes. What happens if they do nothing? Show the real cost — emotional, financial, or practical — of leaving the problem unsolved.
S
Solution
Introduce your answer. Present it clearly and simply, explaining what makes your approach different from what they've already tried.
T
Testimony
Prove it works. Share a before-and-after story — your own or someone else's. Real transformation, told honestly, builds trust faster than any claim.
O
Offer
Make it irresistible. Spell out exactly what the reader gets, address their biggest objection, and remove any reason to hesitate.
R
Response
Ask for one clear action. A single, specific next step — small enough to feel easy, clear enough to act on immediately.
Why it works
PASTOR works because it mirrors how people actually make decisions — they feel a pain, they imagine it getting worse, they look for a solution, they need to trust it, then they act. Writing that follows this sequence doesn't feel like marketing. It feels like someone finally gets it.
